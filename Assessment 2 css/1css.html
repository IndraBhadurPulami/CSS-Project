Desuup Indra Bhadur Pulami

My name is Desuup Indra Bhadur Pulami. I belongs to the dragon country name BHUTAN. My village is Jachhu(MUGA), Gewog Gakidling under the Sarpang Dzongkhag.

I have completed class 12. I have took a commerce in 11 and 12 but could not qulified for further studies as I was not that good in studies, but also i managed to get a job in one of the biggest company called FMCL in the Bhutan as a PoL Manager, in contract. I had done work for 2 years but I was not that happy to contune to do job.

After resigning from FMCL I came back to my village and help my parents untill I joined Desuup. While I was in village COVID-19 pandemic hit world. Immediately our government decide to train youth as a Desuup (GUARDIAN OF PEACE) for country benefits as well as for jobless youths. So, I also planned to join as a Desuup to serve the country. IT was a great oppertunity for me to serve the country. As a Desuup I had gained lots of experience and got chance to study as per my interest.

I had got an opportunity to do ICT foundational course as a Desuup from DSP(Desuung Skilling Program). It was a great opportunity for all Desuup who has a interest in various skilling. Those who has a interest can apply for course as per their interested skill. So, I thank our great King for this oppertunity and if given a chance I will serve our King, County and People(Tsa Wa Sum).

My country is the world, and my religion is to do good.
